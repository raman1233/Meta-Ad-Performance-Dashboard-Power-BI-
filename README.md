# 📊 Meta Ad Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project is a **Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Ad Performance Analysis Dashboard** built using **Power BI**.

The dashboard provides a complete funnel view of advertising campaigns — from **Awareness → Engagement → Conversion → Budget Analysis** — helping marketing teams track campaign effectiveness and optimize ROI.

It delivers demographic, geographic, and time-based insights to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this project is to:

* Track advertising campaign performance across Facebook and Instagram.
* Identify the most effective platform.
* Measure engagement and conversion funnel efficiency.
* Optimize budget allocation.
* Understand audience behavior patterns.

---

## 📌 Scope

### ✅ In Scope

* Paid ad campaigns on **Facebook and Instagram**
* Performance metrics and KPI tracking

### ❌ Out of Scope

* Organic engagement
* Messenger and Audience Network campaigns

## 📈 Key KPIs Implemented

| KPI                     | Description                        | Formula                           |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Impressions             | Number of times ads were displayed | Count of Impression events        |
| Clicks                  | Number of ad clicks                | Count of Click events             |
| Shares                  | Number of shares                   | Count of Share events             |
| Comments                | User comments                      | Count of Comment events           |
| Purchases               | Conversions generated              | Count of Purchase events          |
| Engagements             | Total interactions                 | Clicks + Shares + Comments        |
| CTR                     | Click Through Rate                 | (Clicks ÷ Impressions) × 100      |
| Engagement Rate         | Interaction efficiency             | (Engagements ÷ Impressions) × 100 |
| Conversion Rate         | Click to Purchase %                | (Purchases ÷ Clicks) × 100        |
| Purchase Rate           | Impression to Purchase %           | (Purchases ÷ Impressions) × 100   |
| Total Budget            | Total campaign spend               | Sum of campaign budgets           |
| Avg Budget per Campaign | Budget distribution                | Total Budget ÷ Campaign Count     |

## 🔍 Key Insights from Analysis

### 📌 Funnel Performance

* 216K Impressions
* 25.4K Clicks
* CTR: 11.76% (Strong performance)
* Engagement Rate: 13.56%
* 1.3K Purchases
* Conversion Rate: 5.21%
* Purchase Rate: 0.61%

**Insight:**
Strong awareness and engagement, but significant drop in conversion. Funnel optimization is required.

---

### 👩 Audience Insights

* Female users engage more (43%)
* 18–30 age group is most active
* India & Brazil generate high engagement volume
* Germany & UK represent higher purchasing power

---

### ⏰ Time-Based Insights

* Peak engagement during afternoons & evenings
* Certain calendar dates show spikes (campaign events/promotions)

---

### 🎥 Ad Format Performance

* Video Ads → Highest CTR & Conversion Rate
* Stories → Strong performance
* Image & Carousel → Lower conversion efficiency

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. Optimize landing pages to reduce funnel leakage.
2. Implement retargeting strategies for engaged users.
3. Increase budget allocation to Video & Story ads.
4. Schedule campaigns during peak hours.
5. Segment strategies by geography:

   * High volume → India & Brazil
   * High value → Germany & UK
